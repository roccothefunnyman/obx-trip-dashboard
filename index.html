The pinch points (worse at 80–85 mph): the 157-mi mountain stretch between Monroeville and Hagerstown is the tight one. Fill to 100% at Monroeville (you arrive Hagerstown around ~20%), and if it is hot or windy, splash-charge at a PA Turnpike plaza (Sideling Hill or Somerset). On Leg 2, an 80% charge at Newport News reaches the lodging at ~22%; top off at Chesapeake if your projection dips below ~20%.

Before you go

  • Pack the Porsche NACS-to-CCS adapter — Hagerstown, Richmond, Chesapeake, and the whole island lean on Tesla Superchargers.
  • Set up the Tesla app (Charge Your Non-Tesla) and an Electrify America account before leaving.
  • Verify exact kW on the EVgo units at Monroeville and Williamsburg in PlugShare (could be legacy 50 kW; Tesla SC backups are listed for both).
  • Confirm the McLean hotel's charging; if none, plan the overnight top-up at Tysons Corner EA (~2 mi).
  • Map Targets + ≥75 kW chargers for both legs
  • Confirm lodging and build SOC plan for the Macan 4 Electric
